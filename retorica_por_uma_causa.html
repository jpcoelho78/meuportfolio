Rhetoric for a Cause

In this work, we were asked to choose a cause that we wanted to support and, depending on the chosen cause, I had to make a poster with physical materials and create a sentence in support of the poster. The cause I chose was pollution in the oceans so I used the plastic that exists today in the ocean and painted it blue the paint also uses toxic materials that are discarded in the oceans, on the plastic there is an origami in the shape of a boat with a net symbolizing a fishing boat. Making the message of this work be - the man who pollutes the ocean is the same man who ends up fishing and eating his own garbage - Don't feed the oceans.
