About Me

Hi, my name is João, I'm finishing my second year of Communication Design at Escola Superior Artística do Porto. I like Design because it makes me feel free and creative. I have a special interest in discovering and using 3D more as a way of doing my work, this same interest started when I entered college and I was presented with several 3D works.
When I'm not in front of the computer, I like to play sports, especially running on the beach, listening to music or walking around the city.
